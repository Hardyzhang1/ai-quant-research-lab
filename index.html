Execution alpha

Adaptive participation for VWAP-style execution.

This public snapshot summarizes the execution research track at a high level. The model converts rolling trade-profile and liquidity signals into a constrained AC/IS-style schedule, then adjusts minute-level participation around a historical VWAP plan while controlling inventory risk and schedule drift.

Signal layer

Short-horizon market state

Minute-level price and volume-profile features estimate near-term liquidity, impact, and price-pressure conditions without publishing private raw data or training code.

Optimizer layer

AC/IS participation scheduler

Expected cost, inventory risk, progress pressure, and participation caps are balanced to decide whether the next slice should speed up, slow down, or stay close to the baseline trajectory.

Risk layer
